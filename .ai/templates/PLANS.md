# Execution Plan

> Copy this file to `PLANS.md` at the repo root for large or multi-step work.
> Keep it current while the change is in progress.

## Goal

- What concrete outcome are we trying to achieve?

## Why Now

- What user need, architecture need, or delivery risk makes this work timely?

## Constraints

- What must not change?
- What package boundaries, compatibility limits, or rollout constraints apply?

## Non-Goals

- What is explicitly out of scope?

## Affected Areas

- Which apps, packages, docs, prompts, or tests are expected to change?

## Risks

- What could break?
- What is hard to verify?

## Steps

1. Define the first small, testable slice.
2. List each step in execution order.
3. Note the expected verification after each step.

## Verification

- Which commands, tests, fixtures, or manual checks will confirm correctness?

## Progress Log

- `todo` — pending work item
- `doing` — current step
- `done` — completed step with a short note

## Decisions

- Record important decisions and the reasons behind them.

## Open Questions

- Capture unresolved questions here instead of letting them disappear into chat
  history.
